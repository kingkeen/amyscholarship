    BIOLOGY SCHOLARSHIP

    Margaret Amy Crotts/Boone '83 Biology Scholarship

    Margaret Amy Crotts Boone had ambitious goals for herself from a young age. She had a quick mind and a desire for learning and furthered this passion as a student at Hollins University, where she graduated in the class of 1983.

    After receiving her bachelor's degree in Biology, Amy was able to pursue what she loved and what was at the core of her being--helping others. Specifically, Amy helped others through teaching, and always focused on those less fortunate than herself.

    Even while Amy taught science and math in schools, she visited local prisons and tutored inmates to help them receive their GED. Throughout her life and balancing full time teaching positions with raising a family, she always made time to tutor at schools that catered to children who did not have the resources for quality educations.

    The Margaret Amy Crotts Boone Scholarship is one of Amy's ongoing legacies. The scholarship's focus is to assist young women receive they resources they need, as long as they are determined and motivated individuals, so that they can realize their dreams and pursue their passions.

    About the Scholarship

    The Margaret Amy Crotts Boone Scholarship is for exceptional young women who, like Amy, want to pursue their degree at Hollins in Biology in order to realize their dreams.

    The Scholarship has a GPA requirement and can be applied for through Hollins University for rising freshmen.

    How to Apply

    Apply for Margaret Amy Crotts/Boone '83 Biology Scholarship

    If you want to apply for the Margaret Amy Crotts Boone Biology Scholarship, you must meet certain requirements.

    Scholarships are awarded to students with a 3.2 GPA or higher who maintain a 3.0 GPA throughout the scholarship year. Applicants must also be Biology majors. If no Biology majors meet the requirements within a given year, the Scholarship will be awarded to a Math major who meets the same GPA requirements.

    The scholarship is awarded on a yearly basis and can be reapplied for.

    To apply for the Margaret Amy Crotts Boone Biology Scholarship, please contact Hollins University directly.

    Students may contact Mary Jean Sullivan, the Director of Financial Aid, by emailing corrissmj@hollins.edu or by calling 540-362-6332.
